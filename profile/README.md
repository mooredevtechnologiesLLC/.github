## Hi there 👋

[MooreDev Technologies LLC](https://mooredevtechnologies.com/) is an independent software company dedicated to building privacy-centric tools that help people with executive function challenges navigate daily life with greater ease and confidence.

Executive function is the set of mental skills that help us plan, focus, follow instructions, and manage multiple tasks. Challenges with executive function affect millions of people living with ADHD, autism, TBI, anxiety, and many other conditions. Off-the-shelf productivity and lifestyle apps are rarely designed with these users in mind.

Every app we ship starts with empathy: understanding the real friction points that make daily tasks hard, and designing gentle, intuitive solutions that meet people where they are.
